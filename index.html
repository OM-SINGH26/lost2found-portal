Process

How Lost2Found Works

Three simple steps to reunite students with their belongings.

📋

Report the Item

Submit a detailed report — whether you lost or found something. Include photos and the campus location for best results.

🔍

Smart Matching

The system matches lost items against found reports and notifies both parties instantly when a match is detected.

🤝

Claim & Collect

Verify your ownership and coordinate with the finder or campus security to safely recover your item.
